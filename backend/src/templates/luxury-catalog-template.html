Our Story
Where
We Begin

We weave more than carpets; we create handcrafted heirlooms that carry the essence of heritage and tradition.

Founded on the principles of quality craftsmanship and timeless design, our journey began with a vision: to transform homes into sanctuaries of beauty and comfort.

Choosing us means investing in a piece of heritage — a symbol of quality and tradition that grows richer with time.

History of Craft
India's
Weaving

Since the Mughal Era

India's rug weaving tradition dates to the 16th century, when Persian artistry fused with local craftsmanship to create something wholly unique.

From Kashmir's silk carpets with intricate Persian motifs to Rajasthan's bold geometric Dhurrie rugs — each region carries its own language of thread and colour.
